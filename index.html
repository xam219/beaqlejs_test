Thanks for your interest in participating in the listening test. The overall test will roughly take 20 minutes. Please take the time and carefully rate every item.

Instructions:
  • Most important: use high quality studio headphones and a good soundcard!
  • Listen through all test files and test sets before you do any ratings to get used to the material.
  • Rate the Naturalness/intelligibility of the test items.


Naturalness Test:
  • Participants rated the naturalness of each system's utterance on a five-point scale, with 1 being the worst and 5 being the best.
  • Excellent (5): Very natural,
  • Good (4): Slightly natural,
  • Fair (3): Roughly natural,
  • Poor (2): Abnormal,
  • Bad (1): Not natural at all.


Intelligibility Test:
  • Participants rated the intelligibility of each system's utterance on a five-point scale, with 1 being the worst and 5 being the best.
  • Excellent (5): Complete understanding of every word,
  • Good (4): Slight difficulty with a few words but overall understanding,
  • Fair (3): Understanding roughly half the words,
  • Poor (2): Understanding only a few words,
  • Bad (1): No understanding at all.
